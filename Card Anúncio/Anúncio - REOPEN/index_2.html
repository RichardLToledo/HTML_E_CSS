Descrição

This Wonderful Updated Winnetka home with an entertainers open floor plan located in a quiet family neighborhood has 4 bedrooms & 2 full baths. The home has 1988 sqft situated on a 7487 sqft lot. The spacious sunlit living room has a gorgeous floor to ceiling stacked stone fireplace with raised stacked stone and marble hearth and opens to the wonderful chef's kitchen. The family chef will love the kitchen with ample cabinets, countertop range, double self-cleaning oven, dishwasher, stainless steel vent hood, granite counters, granite back splash, refrigerator is included, laminate floors & convenient breakfast bar. The master suite has an attached sitting room, private remodeled full bathroom & mirrored closet doors. Some of the many amenities are beautifully designed drought resistant landscaping front & rear, copper plumbing, new laminate floors, central air/heat, mostly dual pane windows, water retention system, new front door, recessed lighting, Swan security system & ring door, fully remodeled designer bathrooms with Jacuzzi tub rainfall shower with multiple spray shower heads & new vanities. You'll enjoy hours of entertaining in the private backyard with a covered patio and detached garden shed. There is a 2 car attached garage with brick lined concrete oversized driveway. Close to schools, Pierce College, world class shopping at the Village in Warner Center and convenient to the Orange Line Transportation & freeways.
